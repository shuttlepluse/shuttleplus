# Shuttle Plus - Pricing Guide

## Pricing Structure

**Effective Date:** December 2025
**Currency:** USD (Primary), ETB (Local)
**Minimum Fare:** $25

---

## Vehicle Categories

### Standard Sedan - Base Rate
- **Vehicles:** Toyota Corolla, Hyundai Elantra or similar
- **Capacity:** Up to 3 passengers
- **Luggage:** 2 large suitcases
- **Best For:** Solo travelers, couples

### Executive Sedan - +40% Premium
- **Vehicles:** Toyota Camry, Honda Accord or similar
- **Capacity:** Up to 3 passengers
- **Luggage:** 3 large suitcases
- **Best For:** Business travelers

### SUV/Minivan - +80% Premium
- **Vehicles:** Toyota Fortuner, Hyundai H1 or similar
- **Capacity:** Up to 6 passengers
- **Luggage:** 5 large suitcases
- **Best For:** Families, groups

### Luxury Class - +160% Premium
- **Vehicles:** Mercedes E-Class, BMW 5 Series or similar
- **Capacity:** Up to 3 passengers
- **Luggage:** 3 large suitcases
- **Best For:** VIP clients, special occasions
