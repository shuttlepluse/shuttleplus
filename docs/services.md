# Shuttle Plus - Services Guide

## Core Services

### 1. Airport Arrival Transfers

**Service Description:**
Professional meet & greet service for passengers arriving at Bole International Airport.

**Process:**
1. Driver monitors flight status in real-time
2. Arrives at airport before flight landing
3. Meets passenger at arrivals hall with name sign
4. Assists with luggage
5. Escorts to vehicle
6. Direct transfer to destination

**Included:**
- Flight tracking (automatic delay adjustment)
- 60 minutes free waiting time
- Meet & Greet inside terminal
- Luggage assistance
- Bottled water
- Child seat (on request)

### 2. Airport Departure Transfers

**Service Description:**
Reliable pickup service for passengers departing from any location in Addis Ababa.

**Process:**
1. Driver arrives 10 minutes early
2. Assists with luggage loading
3. Direct transfer to airport
4. Drop-off at departure terminal
5. Luggage assistance at curb

**Recommendations:**
- International flights: Pickup 3.5 hours before
- Domestic flights: Pickup 2 hours before
