# Shuttle Plus - Company Overview

## About Shuttle Plus

Shuttle Plus is a premium airport transfer service operating in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, providing reliable and professional transportation to and from Bole International Airport (ADD).

## Company Information

- **Company Name:** Shuttle Plus
- **Service Type:** Airport Transfer & Ground Transportation
- **Primary Location:** Addis Ababa, Ethiopia
- **Airport Served:** Bole International Airport (ADD)

## Mission Statement

To provide safe, reliable, and comfortable airport transfer services that exceed customer expectations while contributing to Ethiopia's tourism and business travel infrastructure.

## Core Values

1. **Reliability** - On-time pickups with flight tracking
2. **Safety** - Well-maintained vehicles and professional drivers
3. **Comfort** - Premium vehicles with amenities
4. **Transparency** - Clear pricing with no hidden fees
5. **Customer Focus** - 24/7 support and personalized service

## Service Hours

- **Operations:** 24 hours, 7 days a week
- **Customer Support:** 24/7 availability
- **Booking:** Online booking available anytime

## Target Markets

### Primary Customers
- International tourists visiting Ethiopia
- Business travelers
- Diplomats and government officials
- Airline crew members
- Conference attendees

### Secondary Customers
- Local residents with airport needs
- Corporate clients with regular travel
- Travel agencies and tour operators
- Hotels requiring guest transfers

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Flight Tracking Integration** - Automatic adjustment for delays
2. **Meet & Greet Service** - Driver meets passengers inside terminal
3. **Multi-language Support** - English-speaking drivers
4. **Flexible Payment** - USD, ETB, cards, mobile money
5. **Premium Fleet** - Well-maintained modern vehicles
6. **Transparent Pricing** - Fixed rates, no surge pricing
