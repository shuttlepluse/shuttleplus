# Shuttle Plus - Destinations Guide

## Bole International Airport (ADD)

### Airport Information
- **IATA Code:** ADD
- **Full Name:** Addis Ababa Bole International Airport
- **Location:** 6 km southeast of Addis Ababa city center
- **Terminals:** 2 (Terminal 1 - Domestic, Terminal 2 - International)
- **Hub For:** Ethiopian Airlines

### Airport Facilities
- Duty-free shopping
- Currency exchange
- ATMs
- Restaurants and cafes
- VIP lounges
- WiFi available

## Out of City Destinations

### Debre Zeit (Bishoftu)
- **Distance:** 47 km from airport
- **Transfer Time:** 45-60 minutes
- **Notes:** Lake resort town, popular weekend destination
- **Attractions:** Crater lakes, resorts, birdwatching

### Adama (Nazret)
- **Distance:** 99 km from airport
- **Transfer Time:** 1.5-2 hours
- **Notes:** Major industrial city, on route to eastern Ethiopia
- **Attractions:** Hot springs, regional commerce

### Ambo
- **Distance:** 126 km from airport
- **Transfer Time:** 2-2.5 hours
- **Notes:** Known for mineral water springs
- **Attractions:** Natural hot springs, scenic drive

## Traffic Considerations

### Peak Hours (Longer Transfer Times)
- **Morning Rush:** 7:00 AM - 9:30 AM
- **Evening Rush:** 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- **Market Days:** Saturday mornings

### Best Travel Times
- **Early Morning:** Before 7:00 AM
- **Mid-Day:** 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- **Late Evening:** After 8:30 PM
- **Sundays:** Generally lighter traffic

### Special Considerations
- Major holidays may affect traffic
- Construction zones can cause delays
- Rainy season (June-September) may slow travel
