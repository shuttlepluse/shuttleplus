Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an airport transfer?
You can book your transfer online through our website, via WhatsApp, or by calling our 24/7 hotline. Simply provide your flight details, pickup/dropoff locations, and preferred vehicle type.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), mobile payments (Telebirr, CBE Birr), bank transfers, and cash. Corporate accounts can pay via invoice.
What if my flight is delayed?
We track all flights in real-time and automatically adjust your pickup time based on your flight's actual arrival. There's no extra charge for flight delays.
Can I cancel or modify my booking?
Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 4 hours before the scheduled pickup time for a full refund. Changes can be made through our website or by contacting support.
