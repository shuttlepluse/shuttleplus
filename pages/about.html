About Shuttle Plus

Ethiopia's most trusted airport transfer service, connecting travelers with reliable, comfortable transportation since 2019.

Our Story

Shuttle Plus was founded in 2019 with a simple mission: to transform the airport transfer experience in Ethiopia. We noticed that travelers arriving at Bole International Airport often faced uncertainty, negotiations, and unreliable transportation options.

We set out to change that by creating a service that prioritizes transparency, reliability, and comfort. Today, we're proud to be the leading airport transfer provider in Addis Ababa, serving thousands of satisfied customers each month.

Our journey has been marked by continuous improvement, always listening to our customers and adapting to their needs. From our first vehicle to our current fleet of premium cars, we've never compromised on quality.

Our Mission

To provide world-class airport transfer services that exceed expectations. We believe every journey should start and end with comfort, safety, and peace of mind.

We're committed to:

  • Transparent, fixed pricing with no hidden fees
  • Professional, vetted drivers who know the city
  • Modern, well-maintained vehicles
  • 24/7 customer support in multiple languages
  • Real-time flight tracking for timely pickups

Our Core Values

Safety First

Your safety is our top priority. All our drivers undergo rigorous background checks and our vehicles are regularly inspected and maintained.

Punctuality

We track your flight in real-time and adjust pickup times accordingly. Our drivers are always on time, every time.

Customer Care

From booking to drop-off, we're here for you. Our 24/7 support team speaks English, Amharic, and French.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do. Our 4.9-star rating reflects our commitment to quality service.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact with fuel-efficient vehicles and carbon offset programs.

Community

We believe in giving back. We support local communities and provide fair employment to Ethiopian drivers.
