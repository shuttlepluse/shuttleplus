2. Our Services

Shuttle Plus provides airport transfer services in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, including:

  • Airport pickup services from Bole International Airport (ADD)
  • Airport drop-off services to Bole International Airport
  • Meet and greet services at the arrivals hall
  • Hourly charter services within Addis Ababa
  • Corporate transportation solutions
Service Area

Our primary service area covers Addis Ababa and surrounding regions within 100km of Bole International Airport.

3. Booking & Reservations

3.1 Making a Booking

Bookings can be made through our website, mobile app, phone, or WhatsApp. All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation.

3.2 Booking Information

You must provide accurate and complete information when making a booking, including:

  • Full name of the lead passenger
  • Valid contact information (phone and email)
  • Accurate flight details for airport transfers
  • Correct pickup/drop-off addresses
  • Number of passengers and luggage

3.3 Booking Confirmation

A booking is only confirmed when you receive a confirmation email or SMS with your booking reference number. Please verify all details in your confirmation.

4. Payment Terms

4.1 Pricing

All prices are quoted in Ethiopian Birr (ETB) or US Dollars (USD) and include:

  • Vehicle and driver service
  • Meet and greet at arrivals (for airport pickups)
  • 60 minutes of free waiting time
  • Flight tracking service
  • Luggage assistance

4.2 Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Mobile money (Telebirr, CBE Birr)
  • Bank transfer
  • Cash payment to driver
  • Corporate account invoicing

4.3 Additional Charges

Additional charges may apply for:

  • Waiting time exceeding 60 minutes
  • Additional stops not included in original booking
  • Oversized luggage or special equipment
  • Late-night surcharges (11 PM - 5 AM)
  • Vehicle upgrades requested at pickup
