Cancellation Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2025

Flexible Cancellation

Cancel up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup for a full refund. No questions asked!

Cancellation Timeframes

Refund amounts depend on when you cancel relative to your scheduled pickup time:

Cancellation Time Refund Amount Processing Time
More than 24 hours before pickup 100% Full Refund 3-5 business days
12-24 hours before pickup 75% Refund 3-5 business days
2-12 hours before pickup 50% Refund 3-5 business days
Less than 2 hours before pickup No Refund N/A
No-show No Refund N/A

How to Cancel

You can cancel your booking through any of these methods:

Online (Recommended)

  1. Go to My Tickets page
  2. Enter your booking reference and email
  3. Click "Cancel Booking"
  4. Confirm the cancellation
  5. Receive confirmation email with refund details

Via Email

Send an email to bookings@shuttleplus.com with:

  • Subject: "Cancellation Request - [Your Booking Reference]"
  • Your booking reference number
  • Registered email address
  • Reason for cancellation (optional)

By Phone/WhatsApp

  • Phone: +251 91 234 5678
  • WhatsApp: +251 91 234 5678
  • Available 24/7

Refund Processing

Refund Methods

Refunds are processed to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-10 business days to appear on statement
  • Mobile Money (Telebirr/CBE Birr): 1-2 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
  • Corporate Account: Credit applied to next invoice
Refund Confirmation

You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed, including the expected arrival time.

Special Circumstances

Flight Cancellations

If your flight is cancelled by the airline:

  • Contact us immediately with your booking reference
  • Provide airline notification/proof of cancellation
  • Choose to reschedule to your new flight (free of charge) or receive a full refund
  • Full refund applies regardless of cancellation timing

Flight Delays

Flight delays are NOT considered cancellations:

  • We automatically track your flight and adjust pickup time
  • No action needed from you
  • No cancellation or rebooking required
  • Your driver will be there when you land

Shuttle Plus Cancellations

In the rare event that we need to cancel your booking:

  • You'll receive immediate notification via call, SMS, and email
  • Full refund processed automatically
  • We'll attempt to arrange alternative transportation
  • Compensation may be offered for inconvenience

Force Majeure

In cases of extraordinary circumstances beyond our control (natural disasters, civil unrest, pandemic restrictions, etc.):

  • Full refunds or free rebooking will be offered
  • We'll communicate options clearly and promptly
  • Customer safety is our priority

Modifications Instead of Cancellation

Before cancelling, consider modifying your booking instead:

  • Date/Time Changes: Free if made 24+ hours before pickup
  • Destination Changes: Difference in fare applies
  • Vehicle Upgrade: Pay the difference only
  • Add Passengers: May require vehicle upgrade
Changes Within 24 Hours

Modifications within 24 hours of pickup are subject to availability and may incur a small administrative fee.

No-Show Policy

A booking is considered a "no-show" when:

  • You don't arrive at the pickup location
  • You cannot be contacted after the free waiting period
  • You arrive more than 60 minutes late without communication

No-shows are non-refundable as the driver and vehicle are reserved exclusively for you.

Partial Trip Cancellations

If you end a trip early or decide not to continue:

  • Full fare applies for booked transfer
  • No partial refunds for shortened trips
  • You may request to change destination mid-trip (additional charges may apply)

Disputes

If you disagree with a cancellation charge or refund amount:

  1. Contact our support team within 7 days
  2. Provide your booking reference and details
  3. We'll review your case within 48 hours
  4. Decision will be communicated via email
