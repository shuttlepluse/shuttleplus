Driver Guidelines

Professional standards for delivering exceptional airport transfer services

Our Commitment to Excellence

These guidelines ensure every Shuttle Plus passenger receives safe, reliable, and professional transportation. Adherence to these standards is mandatory for all drivers.

Driver Requirements

Eligibility

To become a Shuttle Plus driver, you must:

  • Be at least 25 years of age
  • Hold a valid Ethiopian driver's license for minimum 5 years
  • Have no major traffic violations in the past 3 years
  • Pass a comprehensive background check
  • Complete the Shuttle Plus driver training program
  • Speak fluent English and Amharic
  • Have a smartphone capable of running our driver app

Required Documents

  • Valid driver's license
  • National ID (Kebele ID)
  • Vehicle registration certificate
  • Comprehensive vehicle insurance
  • Vehicle inspection certificate (Bolo)
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Two passport-sized photographs

Professional Conduct

Appearance & Dress Code

  • Wear clean, professional attire (collared shirt recommended)
  • Maintain good personal hygiene
  • Avoid strong perfumes or colognes
  • Shuttle Plus ID badge must be visible at all times
  • No casual wear (shorts, flip-flops, tank tops)

Customer Interaction

  • Greet passengers warmly and professionally
  • Hold a name sign for airport pickups
  • Offer assistance with luggage
  • Open doors for passengers when appropriate
  • Maintain a friendly but professional demeanor
  • Respect passenger privacy and preferences
  • Never discuss controversial topics (politics, religion)
  • Ask before adjusting temperature or music
Zero Tolerance Policy

Shuttle Plus has zero tolerance for discrimination, harassment, or inappropriate behavior toward passengers. Violations result in immediate termination.

Communication

  • Respond promptly to dispatch and passenger messages
  • Confirm arrival at pickup location
  • Notify passengers and dispatch of any delays
  • Keep phone conversations brief while driving
  • Use hands-free devices for calls

Vehicle Standards

Vehicle Requirements

All Shuttle Plus vehicles must:

  • Be model year 2018 or newer
  • Pass annual vehicle inspection
  • Have working air conditioning
  • Have all seatbelts in working condition
  • Be free of dents, scratches, and damage
  • Have tinted windows (within legal limits)
  • Meet emission standards

Daily Vehicle Checklist

Before each shift, verify:

  • Fuel level (minimum half tank)
  • Tire pressure and condition
  • All lights functioning
  • Windshield wipers working
  • Brakes responsive
  • Horn working
  • First aid kit present
  • Fire extinguisher present and valid

Cleanliness Standards

  • Exterior: Washed and polished daily
  • Interior: Vacuumed, dusted, and wiped down daily
  • Windows: Clean inside and out
  • No food odors or smoking residue
  • Fresh, subtle air freshener only
  • Trunk clean and spacious for luggage

Required In-Vehicle Items

  • Phone charger (multi-type cables)
  • Bottled water for passengers
  • Tissues
  • Umbrella
  • City map (backup)
  • First aid kit
  • Fire extinguisher
  • Reflective triangles

Service Protocols

Airport Pickups

  1. Monitor flight status and arrive at airport 15 minutes before landing
  2. Park in designated waiting area
  3. Proceed to arrivals hall with name sign when flight lands
  4. Position yourself in clear view near the exit
  5. Contact passenger if not found within 20 minutes
  6. Assist with luggage and escort to vehicle
  7. Confirm destination before departing

Hotel/Residence Pickups

  1. Arrive 10 minutes before scheduled time
  2. Park safely without blocking traffic
  3. Call/message passenger upon arrival
  4. Wait at the main entrance or specified location
  5. Assist with luggage
  6. Confirm flight details for airport drop-offs

During the Trip

  • Follow GPS navigation and optimal routes
  • Obey all traffic laws
  • Drive smoothly - avoid harsh braking/acceleration
  • Keep radio volume low or off unless requested
  • Inform passengers of estimated arrival time
  • Alert passengers of any significant delays

Trip Completion

  • Stop at a safe, convenient location
  • Help unload luggage
  • Thank the passenger for choosing Shuttle Plus
  • Ensure nothing is left behind
  • Complete trip in the driver app

Safety Guidelines

Driving Safety

  • Always wear seatbelt
  • Ensure all passengers wear seatbelts
  • Never use phone while driving (except hands-free)
  • Maintain safe following distances
  • Adjust speed for road and weather conditions
  • Never drive under influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Take breaks on long trips to avoid fatigue

Emergency Procedures

In case of emergency:

  1. Ensure passenger safety first
  2. Move vehicle to safe location if possible
  3. Call emergency services if needed (911/991)
  4. Contact Shuttle Plus dispatch immediately
  5. Document incident with photos if safe to do so
  6. Obtain witness information if applicable
  7. Complete incident report within 24 hours
Emergency Contacts

Police: 991 | Ambulance: 907 | Fire: 939 | Shuttle Plus Emergency: +251 91 234 5678

Accident Protocol

  • Do not admit fault at the scene
  • Exchange information with other parties
  • Document damage with photos
  • Obtain police report if required
  • Report to Shuttle Plus within 1 hour

Rating & Performance

Performance Metrics

Drivers are evaluated on:

  • Passenger Ratings: Maintain 4.5+ star average
  • Acceptance Rate: Accept 85%+ of trip requests
  • Completion Rate: Complete 95%+ of accepted trips
  • On-Time Arrivals: 95%+ punctuality
  • Response Time: Respond to requests within 60 seconds

Rewards

  • Monthly bonuses for top-rated drivers
  • Priority trip assignments
  • Annual recognition awards
  • Training and development opportunities

Consequences

Failure to meet standards may result in:

  • Warning notice
  • Temporary suspension
  • Required retraining
  • Permanent deactivation

  • Regular training sessions
  • Community forums and events
  • Accident and legal assistance
