Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Shuttle Plus services

Booking Questions

How do I book an airport transfer?

You can book a transfer through our website by clicking "Book Transfer", through our mobile app, by calling +251 91 234 5678, or via WhatsApp. Simply provide your flight details, pickup/drop-off location, and passenger information. You'll receive instant confirmation with your booking reference.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee availability, especially during peak travel seasons. However, we also accept same-day bookings subject to driver availability. For large groups or special vehicles, please book at least 48-72 hours ahead.

Can I book a round-trip transfer?

Yes! You can book both airport pickup and drop-off in a single booking. Simply select "Round Trip" during the booking process. You'll receive a 10% discount on the total fare for round-trip bookings.

Can I book for someone else?

Absolutely. When booking, simply enter the passenger's details instead of your own. Make sure to provide accurate contact information for the traveler so they receive booking updates and can communicate with the driver.

How do I modify my booking?

You can modify your booking through the "My Tickets" page on our website, by contacting our support team, or replying to your confirmation email. Changes are free up to 24 hours before pickup. Changes within 24 hours may incur a small fee depending on availability.

Payment Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods: Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), mobile money (Telebirr, CBE Birr), bank transfer, and cash payment to the driver. Corporate accounts can also pay via monthly invoicing.

When am I charged for my booking?

For card payments, you can choose to pay immediately when booking or pay the driver in cash/mobile money upon arrival. For online payments, your card is charged at the time of booking. Corporate accounts are invoiced monthly.

Are your prices fixed or estimated?

Our prices are fixed and confirmed at booking. The price you see is the price you pay - no surge pricing, no hidden fees. The only exceptions are additional charges for waiting time beyond 60 minutes, extra stops, or vehicle upgrades requested at pickup.

Can I get a receipt for my trip?

Yes, a digital receipt is automatically sent to your email after trip completion. You can also download receipts from the "My Tickets" page. For corporate accounts, detailed invoices are provided monthly with all trip records.

Do you accept foreign currency?

Online payments can be made in USD or ETB. For cash payments to drivers, we prefer Ethiopian Birr. Some drivers may accept USD cash at the current exchange rate, but this is not guaranteed. We recommend using card payment or having local currency.

Service Questions

What if my flight is delayed?

We automatically track your flight! If your flight is delayed, we adjust your pickup time accordingly at no extra charge. Your driver will be waiting when you actually land, not when you were originally scheduled to arrive. You don't need to do anything - we monitor all flights in real-time.

How will I find my driver at the airport?

After clearing customs and collecting your luggage, proceed to the arrivals hall. Your driver will be waiting with a sign displaying your name. You'll also receive the driver's name, photo, phone number, and vehicle details via SMS/email before arrival. Free airport WiFi is available to contact your driver.

How much luggage can I bring?

Luggage allowance depends on your vehicle class:
Standard Sedan: 2 large suitcases + hand luggage
Executive Sedan: 3 large suitcases + hand luggage
SUV/Minivan: 5 large suitcases + hand luggage
Luxury: 3 large suitcases + hand luggage
For excess luggage, consider upgrading your vehicle or contact us for a custom solution.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, we offer child seats and booster seats upon request. Please specify the child's age and weight when booking so we can provide the appropriate seat. Child seats are available at a small additional charge of 150 ETB per trip.

Can I make stops along the way?

Yes, you can request additional stops during your transfer. Please inform us at booking if possible. Each additional stop is charged at 200-300 ETB depending on the detour distance. Quick stops (under 5 minutes) like ATMs are usually free.

Do drivers speak English?

All Shuttle Plus drivers speak English and Amharic. Many also speak additional languages. If you need a driver who speaks a specific language, please mention it when booking and we'll do our best to accommodate.

What's included in the free waiting time?

For airport pickups, you get 60 minutes of free waiting time starting from when your flight lands (not the scheduled time). This covers immigration, baggage claim, and customs. For hotel pickups, you get 15 minutes of free waiting time. Additional waiting is charged at 50 ETB per 15 minutes.

Cancellation Questions

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking. Cancellations made more than 24 hours before pickup are fully refundable. Cancellations within 24 hours receive a 50% refund. No-shows or cancellations within 2 hours are non-refundable. See our Cancellation Policy for details.

How do I cancel my booking?

You can cancel through the "My Tickets" page on our website using your booking reference, by replying to your confirmation email, by calling +251 91 234 5678, or via WhatsApp. You'll receive a cancellation confirmation via email.

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The time it takes to appear in your account depends on your payment method: Credit cards (5-10 business days), mobile money (1-2 business days), bank transfer (3-5 business days).

What if my flight is cancelled?

If your flight is cancelled by the airline, contact us immediately with your booking reference. We'll either reschedule your transfer to your new flight at no extra charge, or provide a full refund regardless of the cancellation timing.

Safety Questions

Are your drivers vetted?

Yes, all Shuttle Plus drivers undergo thorough background checks, including criminal record verification, driving history review, and document verification. Drivers must have at least 5 years of professional driving experience and complete our training program.

Are your vehicles insured?

All vehicles in our fleet carry comprehensive insurance covering passengers, third parties, and luggage. Our insurance meets or exceeds Ethiopian legal requirements. Details are available upon request.

How can I share my trip details?

For your safety, you can share your trip details with friends or family. Your booking confirmation includes a link that shows your driver details and vehicle information. During the trip, you can share your live location through WhatsApp or other apps.

What safety measures are in vehicles?

All vehicles are equipped with working seatbelts, first aid kits, fire extinguishers, and GPS tracking. Vehicles are regularly inspected and maintained. We also maintain high cleanliness standards with regular sanitization.
