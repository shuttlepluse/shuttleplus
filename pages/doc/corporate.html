Why Choose Corporate Account?

A Shuttle Plus Corporate Account simplifies transportation management for your organization with dedicated support, flexible billing, and preferential rates.

Monthly Invoicing

Consolidated billing in ETB or USD. No individual payments needed for each trip.

Dedicated Manager

Personal account manager for bookings, queries, and priority support.

Travel Reports

Detailed monthly reports for expense tracking, compliance, and auditing.

Volume Discounts

Preferential rates based on monthly trip volume. Save up to 20%.

Account Features

Booking Management

  • Multi-user Access: Add multiple authorized bookers within your organization
  • Booking Portal: Dedicated online portal for quick bookings
  • Priority Booking: 24/7 priority booking line for urgent requests
  • Bulk Bookings: Schedule multiple transfers for conferences and events
  • Recurring Trips: Set up regular scheduled transfers

Billing & Payments

  • Flexible Billing: Monthly, bi-weekly, or project-based invoicing
  • Currency Options: Invoice in ETB, USD, or EUR
  • Cost Centers: Allocate trips to different departments or projects
  • PO Numbers: Include purchase order references on invoices
  • Payment Terms: Net 15, 30, or 45 day terms available

Reporting & Analytics

  • Trip Reports: Detailed breakdown of all trips by date, user, and cost
  • Expense Reports: Ready-to-submit reports for finance teams
  • Usage Analytics: Identify travel patterns and optimize spending
  • CO2 Reports: Carbon footprint tracking for sustainability reporting

Getting Started

Setting up a corporate account is simple:

  1. Contact Us: Email corporate@shuttleplus.com or call +251 11 234 5678
  2. Consultation: We'll understand your transportation needs and usage patterns
  3. Documentation: Provide business registration and authorized signer details
  4. Agreement: Review and sign the corporate service agreement
  5. Setup: We'll set up your account, portal access, and user credentials
  6. Training: Brief onboarding session for your team on using the system
Quick Approval

Most corporate accounts are approved within 2-3 business days. Expedited approval available for established organizations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a minimum commitment?

We recommend a minimum of 10 trips per month to benefit from corporate pricing. However, we can discuss flexible arrangements for organizations with variable needs.

Can we cancel the corporate agreement?

Corporate agreements can be terminated with 30 days written notice. Any outstanding invoices must be settled before account closure.

Do you offer contracts for events/conferences?

Yes! We offer special event packages for conferences, workshops, and large gatherings. Contact us with your event details for a custom proposal.
